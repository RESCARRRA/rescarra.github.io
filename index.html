B I O

I find myself situated at the intersection of art and science — as exemplified through my work in the cognitive/behavioral sciences and visual/critical studies. I strive to find effective and transformative solutions through research and design that enrich the experiences of others. Above all, I wish to better the lives of those less fortunate, focusing on the vulnerable and often times overlooked members of our society. I believe science, art, and technology — when designed, developed, and implemented in collaboration with communities — can bring about meaningful, first-order change.

I have worked in academic research for several years, most recently serving as lab manager for the Emotion and Cognition Laboratory @ DPU. Currently, I'm a graduate student at DePaul University's CDM School of Computing and a student at Northwestern University, focusing on Human-Computer Interaction, Software Engineering, and Web Development, respectively.

I currently live in Chicago with my partner, two dogs, and cat. When I'm not coding, studying, writing, or reading I enjoy running, cooking, yoga, and playing games ~ from chess to Skyrim. I am always interested in hearing about what other people are up to :) so, please feel free to share your story, insights, and/or ideas with me! Psst..I also love to collaborate :)
