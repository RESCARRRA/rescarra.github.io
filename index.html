A B O U T

Commited to working smart.

Ryn Escarra-Cypher is a creative problem solver and consultant who offers personal and professional advice to executives and solopreneurs in need of specialized insight to solve complex problems. After years in academic research and analytics, she has a deep understanding of how attitudes, behaviors, and contexts shape outcomes.

Ryn sees clients challenged by complexity in developing and executing relevant strategy effectively. Thus, she works to guide them toward responding, not reacting — finding the ideal balance and a clear path forward. She has extensive experience in data science, qualitative and quantitative research methods, web development, and Agile methodology.

Ryn holds a BA in psychology from DePaul University and has completed graduate studies in information sciences at DePaul University, Northwestern University, and The University of Chicago (Fall 2018).
