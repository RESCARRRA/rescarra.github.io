BRILLIANT WORK.

E/C is invested in solving problems and delivering value through strategic research consulting, project management, 1:1 development.

After years in academic research, analytics, and design, Ryn Escarra-Cypher developed a deep understanding of how attitudes, behaviors, and contexts shape outcomes. She founded E/C on the belief in the power of research and design to bring about meaningful change.

E/C takes unique approach to better understand and inform the design, development, and execution of relevant, effective strategy. Partnering with non-profit and for-profit teams, E/C is eager to get behind projects that share in their commitment to social good.

Ryn holds a BA in industrial-organizational psychology from DePaul University and has completed graduate studies in human-computer interaction, web technologies, & information sciences at DePaul University, Northwestern University, and The University of Chicago.
