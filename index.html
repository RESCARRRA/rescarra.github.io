Background.

Ryn Escarra-Cypher has a deep understanding of the intricacy and importance of our experiences – and how attitudes, behaviors, and contexts shape these accounts and outcomes. She founded E/C on the belief in the power of research & design to bring about meaningful change.

E/C takes a unique approach to better inform the development and execution of relevant, effective strategy. Partnering with for- and not-for-profit teams, organizations, start-ups and solopreneurs, Ryn is eager to get behind projects that share a commitment to social good and demonstrably deliver on that promise.

Ryn has an extensive background in academic research, data science, and design. She holds a BA in industrial-organizational psychology an MS in human-computer interaction from DePaul University, and has completed graduate studies web technologies, and information sciences at Northwestern University and The University of Chicago.
