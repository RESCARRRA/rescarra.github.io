A B O U T

HCI GRADUATE STUDENT + WEB DEVELOPER

I'm a graduate student at DePaul University's School of Computing focusing on Human-Computer Interaction. I have a certificate in web development from Northwestern University and plan on continuing my work in technology, shifting my focus toward machine learning in the health sciences and informatics domain.

As exemplified by my earlier work in the visual + critical studies, to my later contributions in the cognitive + information sciences, I strive to find effective & transformative solutions through integrative research & design. I believe science, art, and technology — when designed, developed, and implemented in collaboration with diverse minds in diverse contexts — can bring about meaningful, first-order change.

I have been involved in academic research for several years, most recently serving as lab manager for the Emotion + Cognition Laboratory in Chicago, IL. Much of my research to date has focused on marginalized populations, namely older adults and at risk-youth in the Chicagoland area.

I live in Chicago with my partner, two dogs, and cat. When I'm not coding, tutoring, writing, or studying, I enjoy reading, running, cooking, music, practicing yoga (which I used to teach!), and playing games ~ from chess to Skyrim.
